Netscape Tales

"Netscape Tales" is an exhibition of internet artworks from the days of Netscape, pre 2000. This exhibition serves to remind us, or introduce us, to the simpler and older times of website creation. We may be so used to the refined and polished websites of today, which use HTML, CSS, and javscript, or any other combination of languages. But how far can we go with a website just using pure HTML? The results may surprise you. These net artworks take us back to these less complicated times and give us a breath of fresh air.


The File Room

The File Room (1994) by Antoni Muntadas and collaborators is a net artwork that documents cases of censorship, so that such works aren't completely erased and are archived somewhere. Through navigating the website, users are able to submit their own cases of censorship, thus adding onto the artwork themselves.


Form Art

Form Art (1997) by Alexei Shulgin is a website that holds the submissions to a net artwork competition, in which users make artworks out of HTML form elements. The website itself also holds to that rule, with the main page itself being of buttons which leads to pages with buttons, textboxes, and checkboxes. My favorite page of this website being of when some of buttons lead you to a game, also made up entirely of form elements, which I find to be very impressive and unique.


Communication Creates Conflict

Communication Creates Conflict (1995) by Heath Bunting is a net artwork of which takes messages users enter, and has it displayed through different ways, such as being on a sign in a public area, on a fax, or on a postcard. Due to the odd natre of random messages displayed in public areas, they can potentially create interesting social situations, thus the title "Communication Creates Conflict." The main page greets you with a poem, of which some characters of the text are hyperlinked to another page in which the user is taken to a submission form in which to submit the message, accompanied with an excerpt relating to the method in which the user's message will be displayed.
